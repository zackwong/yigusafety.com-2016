● PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

YIGU Anti-Slip Safety Tapes,a aluminium oxide coated,slip-resistant material adhered by a durable epoxy resin to a Acrylic pressure-sensitive adhesive backed PET film.

YIGU Anti-Slip Safety Tapes provide a safe, non-slip surface under shoe traffic to prevent unnecessary slips and falls at any area where slipping is a hazard. A strong PET backing filled with a gritty mineral abrasive to deliver traction on flooring in work areas,stair treads,construction equipment,ramp ways,transportation and recreational vehicles.These tapes are designed for indoor and outdoor use wherever a non-skid surface is required.

● PRT PRODUCTS QUALITY AND FEATURE

PET Anti-slip tape substrate material is PET film,it is an acronym for "polyethylene terephthalate".It is a highly crystaline polymer.The physical mechanical properties of PET perform well in a wide range of temperature.It is resistant to creep,fatigue and abrasion.PET is of fine dimensional stability,good function of environmental protection,harmless and nonpoisonous.

①High performance at tensile strength & peel strength.

②Weatherability,waterpoof,and anti-UV stability.

③Durable mineral abrasive grit with high traction surface.

④Environmental protection material,chemical-free.
