● PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

YIGU Anti-Slip Safety Tapes,a aluminium oxide coated,slip-resistant material adhered by a durable epoxy resin to a Acrylic pressure-sensitive adhesive backed aluminum foil.

YIGU Anti-Slip Safety Tapes provide a safe, non-slip surface under shoe traffic to prevent unnecessary slips and falls at any area where slipping is a hazard. A strong aluminum foil backing filled with a gritty mineral abrasive to deliver traction on flooring in work areas,stair treads,construction equipment,ramp ways,transportation and recreational vehicles.These tapes are designed for indoor and outdoor use wherever a non-skid surface is required.
